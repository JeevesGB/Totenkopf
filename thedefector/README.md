# The Defector
---

The Defector is a mod where you play as a Scientist working deep within a top secret biological research facility who at the later end of the war wishes to defect to the United States.  

---

Within the *thedefector* folder you will see:

```

    bts--
        |
        images

    maps--
          |
          levels
    
    prefabs--
             |
             furnature/props/lights/etc
```
